Who are we?

We are an IV (InterVarsity christian fellowship) small group, and all that means is that we are just a bunch of guys who want to do life together and grow in our faith!

What do we do?

We do lots of things together, some just for fun and some that are more meaningful. We try to foster a community of accountability and encouragement. The central theme of our group is to welcome all and teach them what we have learned about the Lord, and to provide a safe place for people to work through the challenges of emerging adulthood.

Events that we attend/host?

We host and participate in a variety of things...

  • Small Group Hangouts (usually a bible study) on Thursday nights
  • Large Group service (all of IV meets up) on Friday nights
  • One-on-One meetups during the semester to continue to build friendships
  • Accountability partners to help keep us on the straight and narrow
  • Two formal dances with the chapter in spring and fall
  • And other fun events that are put on by the club
